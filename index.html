Welcome to Quarity Labs. We are a blockchain software engineering company dedicated to the Polkadot and JAM ecosystem.

Current Project: Proof of Personhood Marketplace

We are building a marketplace that allows you to lend your "personhood" to others in contexts you don't care about.

Since it's fully anonymous, no one will be able to track it back to the other contexts. Example: you don't care about participating in the Polkadot Forum - you can lend your identity there so that someone else can get "personhood" verification.
